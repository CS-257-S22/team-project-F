Welcome to H-1B Employer Data Hub!

This program allows users to gather information about H1b-Visa administered by the US government.

There are currently two features that you can test at the moment.

    Get Companies By State:
  • Route: /companies/search?state="state"
  • Notice that the "state" is where you put the state.
    The "state" needs to be in a two letter abbreviation => ex) CA, MN
    Get Companies With Minimum Initial Approval:
  • Route: /companies/search?minInitApproval="threshold"
  • Notice that the "threshold" is where you put the number for minimum threshold.
    The "threshold" has to be a number.
