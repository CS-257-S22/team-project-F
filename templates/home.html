Welcome to H-1B Employer Data Hub!

This program allows users to gather information about H1b-Visa administered by the US government.

There are currently two features that you can test at the moment.

    Get Companies By State:
  • Route: /companies/search?state=YourState
  • Notice that the YourState is where you put the state.
    YourState needs to be in a two letter abbreviation => ex) CA, MN
    Get Companies With Minimum Initial Approval:
  • Route: /companies/search?minInitApproval=YourNumber
  • Notice that the YourNumber is where you put the number for minimum threshold.
    YourNumber has to be a number.
